Services / BIM Modeling

BIM Modeling

  • Building Information Modeling, is a leading strategy in project management and construction in sighting.
  • Constructive is the firm that takes BIM Modeling to the far beyond of converting the 2D CAD drawings to a 3D Model.
  • where we visualize the construction site into a visual animation, viewing every detail in the construction procedure.
  • allowing the project team to view all construction models starting from design models to condition modeling up to utility and site features..
  • giving the project heads the ability to overview all project potentialities, from estimated construction duration, cost and resource applications, construction risk fore sighting and management.

4D & 5D BIM Modeling

  • Leading construction fore sighting techniques now connect the 3D BIM models to CPM Schedules where an extra two dimensions are forecasted to provide more accurate construction viewing, where the two dimensions inserted are the time element & cost and resource loading drafted from the Construction CPM Schedule.
  • our integrated 4D & 5D Models shows you a smart detailed viewing for actual construction progress relying on logical elemental connectivity of construction planning.
  • Constructive brings the ultimate complexity into an absolute simplicity in each and every model slide.
  • linking between virtualized CAD drawings, scheduled activities and milestones, within it’s set timing and proper cost and resource allocation.
  • which guarantees project actual control allowing all responsible parties to understand and overview project scopes and spending.
  • We are capable of handling the BIM modeling of your entire project setting up the models of you and your subcontractors, from structural steel to mechanical and electrical plumbing, fire systems, HVAC we model it all.

BIM Modeling Coordination & Clash Detection

  • When the models are ready, coordination is the right next step.
  • Hence, we need to make sure that there is no overlapping between any of the subcontractor’s trades.
  • Clash Detection through coordination allows us to expose construction contradictions before they fall into action during the actual construction progress.
  • avoiding resource squandering and cost inflation.
  • shutting the doors in the face of sub contractual claims too early.

BIM Modeling LOD

  • Constructive are able to view any detail in your BIM Model, starting from building framing to switches.
  • suiting all different types of Models (Design Intent Model, Construction Model, Fabrication Model, Federated Model, As-Built Model, Record Model)
